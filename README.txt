WHAT IS CAMPUS CASH PRO?
--------------------------
Campus Cash Pro is an all-in-one personal finance toolkit designed
for college students. It lives entirely in a single HTML file —
no installation, no subscription, no account required.

Track your monthly budget, log transactions, manage sinking funds,
set savings goals, monitor subscriptions, track debt payoff, log
GPA, record scholarships and internships, and keep a money journal —
all from one file that opens right in your browser.

Your data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to a server.


HOW TO OPEN CAMPUS CASH PRO
------------------------------
1. Find "CampusCashPro.html" in your downloads or project folder.

2. Double-click the file to open it in your default browser.
   It works in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.
   No internet connection required after the first open.

3. Complete the quick setup. The first time you open it, a short
   welcome screen will guide you through entering your name, school,
   and choosing a color theme. Takes about 60 seconds.

4. (Optional) Bookmark it. Drag the file into your browser toolbar
   for quick one-click access.


WHERE IS YOUR DATA STORED?
----------------------------
All your data is stored locally in your browser using "localStorage" —
a storage area on your device managed by your browser.

- Nothing is ever sent to a server or the internet.
- Your data is tied to the specific browser and device you use.
- If you open the file in a different browser or on a different
  device, you will start fresh.

Use the export feature (Settings -> Export Data) to back up your
data and move it between devices.


IMPORTANT: DO NOT USE INCOGNITO / PRIVATE MODE
-------------------------------------------------
Do not use a private browsing or incognito window as your regular
window for Campus Cash Pro.

Private browsing clears all local storage when the window is
closed, which means your data will be permanently lost at the end
of every session.

Always use a normal (non-private) browser window.


BACKING UP YOUR DATA
----------------------
Campus Cash Pro has a built-in backup system.

To export a backup:
  1. Go to Settings (gear icon in the sidebar).
  2. Click "Export Data."
  3. A small .json file will download to your Downloads folder.
  4. Store it in a safe place (cloud folder, external drive, etc.).

To import a backup:
  1. Open Campus Cash Pro in your browser.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Click "Import Data."
  4. Select your previously exported .json file.
  5. Your data will be restored immediately.

Tip: Export a backup once a month, or before switching computers
or browsers.


TROUBLESHOOTING
-----------------

My data disappeared. What happened?
  This usually happens when the file was opened in a private/
  incognito window, or if your browser storage was cleared.
  Going forward, always use a normal browser window.
  If you have a backup .json file, restore it via Settings ->
  Import Data.

The app looks broken or won't load.
  Try a hard refresh:
    Mac:     Cmd + Shift + R
    Windows: Ctrl + Shift + R
  Make sure you are using a modern browser (Chrome 90+, Safari 14+,
  Firefox 88+, or Edge 90+).

Is Campus Cash Pro available on mobile?
  Campus Cash Pro is designed for desktop and laptop use. Some
  features may open in a mobile browser, but the layout and
  experience are optimized for a computer screen. For the best
  results, use a desktop or laptop.

Can I use Campus Cash Pro offline?
  Yes — after the first load it works fully offline. The only
  feature requiring internet is loading the app's fonts. If you are
  offline the app falls back to a system font automatically.

Will my data sync between devices?
  Not automatically. Use the export/import feature in Settings
  to move your data between devices.

Can I rename or move the HTML file?
  Yes. The file name and location don't affect how the app works.
